Risks & Challenges

Honest about the hard parts.

Great hardware is hard. Here is where the risk lives and how we are managing it.

Component supply

The RK3588S and 4K AMOLED have long lead times. We have placed early reservations and designed pin-compatible fallbacks for the most volatile parts.

RF certification

Multi-radio devices face real regulatory hurdles. We have budgeted a full certification window and partnered with an experienced compliance lab.

Schedule slip

Hardware timelines move. We will not ship a half-baked unit to hit a date — and we will post transparent monthly updates either way.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is AXIOM legal to own and use?+
Yes. AXIOM is a maker and security-research tool, like other multi-radio devices on the market. It is built for exploring and auditing wireless systems you own or are authorized to test, and for learning how RF, RFID, and NFC work. You are responsible for using it lawfully in your jurisdiction.
Do I need to assemble it myself?+
No. Every reward tier ships fully assembled and tested. The Maker Kit additionally includes the open BOM, STL files, and schematics if you want to repair, mod, or print your own parts — but that is entirely optional.
What operating system does it run?+
A full Linux desktop tuned for the RK3588S, with the AXIOM launcher for radios and tools layered on top. Because it is open, you can run your own distro, containers, and on-device AI models.
Is the battery replaceable?+
Yes. The 4500 mAh Li-Po sits in a printed holder secured with standard screws into brass inserts. No glue, no proprietary cell — open the shell and swap it.
When will I get mine?+
Founder tiers (Super Early Bird and Standard) are scheduled for Q2 2027; Maker Kit and Founders Edition follow in Q3 2027 as the upgraded materials come online. Every backer gets monthly progress updates.
What if the campaign misses a stretch goal?+
The base AXIOM ships regardless — stretch goals are additive upgrades, not prerequisites. Anything unlocked ships to all backers of a hardware tier.
